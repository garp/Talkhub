## Getting Started

### What is TalkHub?
TalkHub is a social + chat app where you can join hashtag communities, chat in real-time, post updates, share stories, and message people privately.

### Create an account
1) Open the app and tap **Sign up**  
2) Choose **Email** or **Phone**  
3) Enter the OTP code you receive  
4) Complete your profile (name, username, location)  

### Log in
1) Tap **Log in**  
2) Enter your email/phone  
3) Enter the OTP code you receive  

### I didn’t receive the OTP
Try these in order:
- Check spam/junk (email OTP)
- Confirm your phone country code (SMS OTP)
- Wait ~60 seconds and tap **Resend OTP**
- Make sure you can receive SMS on your number (no DND/blocks)

## Account & Profile

### How do I change my profile (photo/bio/location)?
- Go to **Profile** → **Edit** (wording may vary)
- Update your photo, bio, and location and save

### Can I delete my account?
TalkHub currently supports **temporary account deletion**:
- Your profile is hidden, and you can restore the account by logging in again.
- In the app: **Settings** → **Account** → **Delete account (temporary)**  

### I deleted my account — how do I restore it?
Just log in again using the same email/phone (or Google login if you used that). Your account is automatically restored on login.

### I got logged out unexpectedly
This can happen if:
- Your session expired
- Your token was revoked (for example: an admin forced a logout for security)

What to do:
1) Close and reopen the app  
2) Log in again (OTP or Google/Facebook)  
3) If it keeps happening, contact support with your username and the approximate time it occurred

## Chats (Hashtag Chats)

### What are Hashtag chats?
Hashtag chats are community chatrooms tied to a hashtag. Some are:
- **Public**: anyone can join
- **Private**: join by invite/approval
- **Broadcast**: only certain roles can send messages

### How do I join a Hashtag chat?
- Open a hashtag from Search/Feed
- Tap **Join**

### Why can’t I send messages in a hashtag chat?
Possible reasons:
- It’s a **broadcast** hashtag and you don’t have permission to post
- You were removed or restricted by moderators/admins
- Your session needs re-login

### How do I mute a hashtag chat?
- Open the hashtag chat → **Chat settings** → **Mute**
- Choose a duration: **8 hours**, **1 day**, or **Always**

### How do I leave a hashtag chat?
- Open the hashtag chat → **Chat settings** → **Leave**
- If you choose “Leave and delete for me”, it will be hidden from your chat list

## Private Chats (DMs & Groups)

### What’s the difference between a DM and a group?
- **DM**: 1:1 private chat
- **Group**: multiple participants, with admins/moderators

### How do I create a group chat?
- Go to **Messages** → **New group**
- Select participants and confirm

### How do I add/remove people from a group?
Only admins (and sometimes moderators) can manage members.
- Open the group → **Group info** → **Add/Remove participants**

### How do I pin a chat?
- Long-press chat in your chat list → **Pin**

### How do I clear chat messages?
Clearing chat hides older messages for you (it doesn’t necessarily delete for everyone).
- Open chat → **Chat settings** → **Clear messages**

### How do I mute a private chat?
- Open chat → **Chat settings** → **Mute**
- Choose a duration: **8 hours**, **1 day**, or **Always**

### How do I leave a group and keep history?
- Use **Leave group**

### How do I leave a group and remove it from my chat list?
- Use **Leave and delete for me**

## Posts, Feed, and Reposts

### How do I create a post?
- Go to **Create** → **Post**
- Add text and optionally add image/video

### Can I mention someone in a post?
Yes. Type `@username` in your post. Mentioned users receive an in-app notification (and push notification if enabled).

### What is a repost?
A repost lets you share someone else’s post to your followers, optionally with your own commentary.

## Stories

### What are Stories?
Stories are temporary photo/video posts that can be highlighted into collections.

### How do I add a story to highlights?
- Open your story → **Highlight**
- Choose an existing highlight collection or create a new one

## Search & Nearby Places

### How do I search for chats, people, or messages?
Use Search. TalkHub supports a global search that can return:
- Chats (hashtags)
- Chits (messages in chats you’re part of)
- People
- Topics (interest categories)

### What are favourites?
Favourites let you save places (hotels, restaurants, cafes, etc.) to quickly access them later.

## Referrals & Invite Codes

### What is a referral code?
It’s a 6-character code you can share with friends. When someone uses it, they’re linked as your referral.

### How do I get my referral code?
- Go to **Settings** → **Invite friends**
- If you don’t have a code yet, tap **Generate**

### How do I apply someone’s invite code?
- During signup (or later in settings), enter the code and confirm.
Note: invite codes can only be applied once.

## AI Features (Chat, Voice, Image)

### AI Chat: what is it?
AI Chat is a ChatGPT-like chat inside TalkHub with streaming replies and conversation history.

### AI Voice: why is my audio not working?
Common fixes:
- Grant microphone permission
- Make sure Bluetooth/headset is connected correctly
- Try switching networks (voice uses real-time streaming)

### AI Image: why did image generation fail?
Common reasons:
- Prompt is empty/too long
- The AI provider is rate-limited
- Temporary network issue  
Try again after 30–60 seconds.

## Notifications & Mute

### I’m not getting push notifications
Check:
- Your system notification permissions for TalkHub
- You’re logged in on the same device
- You didn’t mute the chat/hashtag
- Battery optimizations are not blocking background activity (Android)

### I muted a chat — will I still receive messages?
Yes. Muting suppresses notifications, but messages still arrive inside the app.

## Safety, Blocking, and Reporting

### How do I block someone?
- Open their profile → **Block**

### What does blocking do?
Blocking typically:
- Removes follow connections
- Restricts interactions
- May block or restrict private chatting

### How do I mute someone?
- Open their profile → **Mute**
Muting reduces noise without fully blocking.

## Troubleshooting

### “Invalid token” / “Please login again”
This usually means your session is expired or revoked. Log in again to refresh your session.

### Upload failed (image/video/audio)
Try:
- Switch networks (Wi‑Fi vs mobile data)
- Retry once
- Ensure the file type is supported
- If it keeps failing, contact support with the time and file type/size

### Messages not sending
Try:
- Confirm you’re online
- Reopen the chat
- Log out and log back in
